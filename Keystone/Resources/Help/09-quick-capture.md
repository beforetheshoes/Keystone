# Quick capture

`⌘N` (Mac and iPad with hardware keyboard) or the **+** tab (iPhone) opens **Quick capture** — a focused modal for adding a new record without losing your current spot.

## How it works

1. Press `⌘N`. The capture overlay appears.
2. Type a title in the big text field.
3. Pick a kind from the grid below: **Person**, **Pet**, **Vehicle**, **Document**, **Event**, **Note**.
4. Press Return.

The record is created in the database that matches the kind, and the detail view opens.

## Where each kind lands

| Kind     | Goes to        |
|----------|----------------|
| Person   | People         |
| Pet      | Pets           |
| Vehicle  | Vehicles       |
| Document | Documents      |
| Event    | Events & Trips |
| Note     | Documents      |

(Note maps to Documents until a dedicated Notes database exists.)

## Defaults

- Title becomes the typed text.
- Glyph is auto-generated from the first 1–2 initials of the title (`Eleanor Marsh` → `EM`).
- Tone is inherited from the database's accent color.
- All other property values start empty — fill them in on the detail view.

## Cancel

Press Escape, or click outside the overlay. Quick capture closes without creating a record.
